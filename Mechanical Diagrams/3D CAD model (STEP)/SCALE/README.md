## Scale components of the measurement device (S1-S7)

S1 and S2: S1 is the upper plate of the scale. It's meant to be screwed to the load cell used the other end of which should be 
screwed in with S2 and connected to the camera stand(CS) plate CS1.
S3: Component which is connected to the screwholes at the both ends of S1 (one component for the left side and one for the right side)
It is used to hold the plastic cover sheet placed on top of the plant-based meat sample.
S4 and S5: are components which are to be inserted into S3 with the goal of adjusting the height of the plastic cover sheet.
S6: Component which is connected to the S1 and used as a stand onto which the plant-based sample should be placed
in order to dry mostly through the bottom surface.
