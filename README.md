Low-cost plant-based meat moisture content measurement device based on Raspberry Pi 4 B

This depository contains work done at the Optics and Photonics laboratory at Niigata University by a 4th year student Kostas Martynas Balciunas throughout the years 2025 and 2026 for his Bachelor's Thesis.

The depository includes:
- Mechanical Diagrams of the developed device
- Software and programs used for the developed device and its measurement data analysis
- Electronical Diagrams of the developed device
- Others
